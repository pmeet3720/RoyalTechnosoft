   int

    int: Stores integers, typically 4 bytes in size.
    short: Stores smaller integers, usually 2 bytes.
    long: Stores larger integers, typically 4 or 8 bytes.
    long long: Stores very large integers, typically 8 bytes.
    unsigned int: Stores positive integers, typically 4 bytes.
    unsigned short: Stores positive smaller integers, usually 2 bytes.
    unsigned long: Stores positive larger integers, typically 4 or 8 bytes.
    unsigned long long: Stores very large positive integers, typically 8 bytes.

    float: Stores single-precision floating-point numbers, typically 4 bytes.
    double: Stores double-precision floating-point numbers, typically 8 bytes.
    long double: Stores extended-precision floating-point numbers, typically 10 or 16 bytes.
