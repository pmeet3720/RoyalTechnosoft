7.9

(A) WHILE AND DO...WHILE
WHILE: Checks condition before loop.
DO...WHILE: Runs loop once before checking.

(B) WHILE AND FOR
WHILE: Used when iteration count is unknown.
FOR: Best when iteration count is known.

(C) BREAK AND CONTINUE
BREAK: Exits loop/switch immediately.
CONTINUE: Skips to next loop iteration.

(D) BREAK AND GOTO
BREAK: Exits loop or switch.
GOTO: Jumps to labeled code.

(E) CONTINUE AND GOTO
CONTINUE: Skips to next loop cycle.
GOTO: Jumps anywhere in the code.
