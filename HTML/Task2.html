ARMAAN MALIK

 
        Armaan Malik (born 22 July 1995) is an Indian singer, songwriter, record
        producer, voice-over artist, and actor. He is known for his singing in
        multiple Indian languages. He is the brother of music composer Amaal
        Mallik.
